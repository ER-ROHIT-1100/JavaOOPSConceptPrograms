 Defination:-
  The Process of one class aquiring the properties of another class is called as Inheritance.
   OR
.The class sharing the properties is called as super/parent class.
The class aquiring the properties is called as
child/derived class.

RULES:-
.It is achieved with help of "extends" Keyword.
.Only variables and methods can be inherited where blocks and constructor can not be inherited.

Five types of Inheritance are there:-
1.Single level 2.Multi level 3.Hierarchical level
4.Multipl level 5.Hybrid level
